About

  • Lollipop Music is an online music rating platform. With Lollipop Music, you can enter songs, rate, and review all your favorite songs!
  • Have access and contribute to leaderboards of all different genres
  • Create your own personal leaderboard
  • Share your leaderboards with friends!
  • Explore differnt genres!

Here are some of the plans we offer!:

Free Plan

  1. Personalized Stations with Ads
  2. Limited amount of skips per hour
  3. Only one device plays at a time
  4. Can only rate 5 songs a day

Premium $3.99/month

  1. Ad Free Listening
  2. Unlimited Amount of Skips
  3. Can play one account on up to 3 devices at the same time
  4. Rate and Review all your favorite songs with no daily cap
  5. Connect with friends and compare personal leaderboards
